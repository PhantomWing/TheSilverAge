# 1.0.0

Initial release of the project.

Notable features:
### Silver
- Silver Ore generation added to Overworld biomes
  - More common in Deep Dark / Ancient City biomes
- Oxidization mechanic for Silver blocks
  - Block of Silver, Cut Silver, Cut Silver Slab, Cut Silver Stairs, Chiseled Silver, Silver Grate, Silver Door, Silver Trapdoor
- Silver Tools & Armor
  - Silver Sword, Silver Pickaxe, Silver Axe, Silver Shovel, Silver Hoe
  - Silver Helmet, Silver Chestplate, Silver Leggings, Silver Boots
  - Silver Horse Armor
  - Use Silver Ingot to repair your tools & armor
  - Use Silver Ingot as an armor trim material
- Use Silver blocks/ingots to power a Beacon
- Use Silver Nuggets when crafting a Firework Star
- Silverfish now have a chance to drop Silver Nuggets when defeated (Configurable!)

### Vanilla Minecraft Changes
- Rebalanced Minecraft crafting recipes to require Silver (Configurable!)
  - Brewing Stand
  - Redstone Repeater
  - Redstone Comparator
  - Name Tag
  - Lodestone
  - Glistering Melon Slice (now crafted with Silver Nuggets or Gold Nuggets)
- Some textures updated to include Silver details
